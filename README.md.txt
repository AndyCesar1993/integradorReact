<h2>Description</h2>
<p>BikeStore is an ecommerce dedicated to the commercialization of bicycles and their respective accessories. The page has the following sections:</p>

<ul>
  
<li>Products</li>
<p>In this section at first instance you can see all the products we have, and it also has a form to filter the products, if you want any specific.</p>

<li>About Us</li>
<p>The about us section has a brief description of our site and the brands we work with.</p>

<li>Contact</li>
<p>In this section you can find all the information to contact us.</p>

<li>Shop</li>
<p>In this section you add all the products you want to buy, you also have a section to add the shipping options.</p>

<li>User</li>
<p>In the user section, you can register a new user as well as log in.</p>

<li>Orders</li>
<p>For registered users, a section appears in which they can see the details of the orders placed and their status.</p>

</ul>

<h2>Functionality</h2>
<p>Welcome to the frontend repository of my online store, a platform built with Vite.js which offers users the ability to browse and purchase a wide variety of products. Our app allows users to register, add products to the shopping cart, confirm their purchases, and provide shipping data to complete their orders efficiently and securely.</p>
